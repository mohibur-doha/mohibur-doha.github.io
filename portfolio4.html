Who I Am

About Me

I am an Agricultural Engineer specializing in Water Resource Engineering and Irrigation Water Management, with a deep passion for applying geospatial technologies to solve real-world water challenges.

My work sits at the intersection of hydrology, remote sensing, and climate science — using GIS and satellite data to analyze irrigation systems, model climate impacts, and improve water use efficiency in agriculture.

I am driven by the belief that data-informed decisions are key to building resilient, water-secure communities in a changing climate.
